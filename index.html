Barangay Fiesta 2026 — Save the Date!

❤ Health May 3, 2026
Free Medical & Dental Mission — May 10
A free medical and dental mission will be held on May 10, 2026 at the Barangay Hall from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Services include free consultation, blood pressure monitoring, dental extraction, and distribution of free medicines. Bring your valid ID and health card.

🔧 Infrastructure May 3, 2026
Garbage Collection Schedule Updated
Effective May 6, 2026, garbage collection will be every Tuesday and Friday. Place properly segregated bags outside your home by 6:00 AM on collection days. Separate biodegradable, non-biodegradable, and recyclables in distinct bags.

Quarterly Barangay Assembly — May 20
All residents are invited to the Quarterly Barangay Assembly on May 20, 2026 at 2:00 PM at the Barangay Hall. Agenda includes: presentation of 2026 budget utilization, updates on ongoing projects, and open forum. Your attendance and participation matters.

❤ Health Apr 28, 2026
Dengue Prevention — Community Clean-up Brigada
A community clean-up drive to prevent dengue will be conducted on May 9 (Saturday) starting 7:00 AM. All households are urged to eliminate stagnant water. Purok leaders will inspect each household. Free anti-dengue kits will be provided to participating families.
📌 All Puroks Brgy. Health Worker
Alerts & Advisories
Emergency notices and important community alerts
🚨
WEATHER ALERT — Tropical Storm Egay
PAGASA has placed Camarines Sur under Tropical Cyclone Wind Signal #1. Residents in low-lying areas and near rivers are advised to evacuate to the Barangay Emergency Shelter. Secure your properties and prepare emergency kits (food, water, medicines, documents).
Emergency · May 4, 2026, 10:30 AM · Source: PAGASA / Brgy. DRRM
⚠️
Flash Flood Advisory — Low-Lying Areas
Due to continuous rainfall, residents near Sitio Bunga creek and Sitio Burol are warned of possible flash floods. Do not cross flooded pathways. Move valuables to higher ground. Be ready to evacuate on short notice.
Warning · May 4, 2026, 8:00 AM · Source: Brgy. DRRM Team
🔧
Road Repair — Temporary Closure (May 6–8)
The main road along Sitio Lamon (near the elementary school) will undergo repairs from May 6–8, 2026. Traffic will be redirected via the alternate road near the covered court. Please plan your travel accordingly.
Advisory · May 3, 2026 · Source: Brgy. Infrastructure
💧
Water Supply Interruption — May 5
LCWD will conduct maintenance on the main pipeline on May 5, 2026 (Tuesday) from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Water supply will be temporarily cut in all sitios. Please store enough water for your household needs in advance.
Advisory · May 2, 2026 · Source: LCWD / Brgy. Admin
